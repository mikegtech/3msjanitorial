# Content Guidelines

Copy and messaging guidelines for the 3MS Janitorial website.

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Professional** but approachable
- **Confident** but not boastful
- **Helpful** and solution-oriented
- **Local** and community-focused

### Avoid
- Overly casual language ("Hey there!")
- Corporate jargon ("synergize", "leverage")
- Negative competitor comparisons
- Unrealistic promises

## Key Messages

### Primary Value Proposition
> "Professional commercial cleaning you can trust"

### Supporting Messages
1. **Reliability**: "We show up when we say we will"
2. **Thoroughness**: "We don't cut corners"
3. **Trust**: "Insured, bonded, and background-checked"
4. **Local**: "Proudly serving Hunt County businesses"
5. **Flexibility**: "Customized to your needs and schedule"

## Service Descriptions

### Office Cleaning
Focus on: productivity, professionalism, employee health

> Keep your workplace productive and professional with regular cleaning tailored to your schedule.

### Medical Facilities
Focus on: compliance, sanitization, patient safety

> Specialized cleaning protocols that meet healthcare sanitation standards and keep patients safe.

### Retail Spaces
Focus on: customer experience, first impressions, after-hours

> Create a welcoming shopping environment that keeps customers coming back.

### Warehouses
Focus on: scale, safety, industrial capabilities

> Heavy-duty cleaning solutions for distribution centers, factories, and industrial facilities.

## CTA Copy

### Primary CTA
- "Request a Quote" (preferred)
- "Get Your Free Quote"
- "Get Started"

### Secondary CTA
- "Call Now"
- "Call Us Today"

### Avoid
- "Submit" (too generic)
- "Contact Us" (too passive)
- "Learn More" (delays conversion)

## FAQ Content

Structure each FAQ as:
1. Question the user actually has
2. Direct answer first
3. Additional context if needed

**Example**:
> **Q: Do you offer after-hours cleaning?**
>
> Yes! We understand that cleaning during business hours can be disruptive. We offer flexible scheduling including early morning, evening, and weekend cleaning to work around your operations.

## Review Testimonials

### Format
- Quote with specific praise
- Customer first name + last initial
- Business name (with permission)
- 5-star rating

### Good Example
> "3MS Janitorial has been cleaning our dental office for over a year now. They understand the specific needs of a medical environment and never miss a detail." â€” Sarah M., Greenville Family Dental

### Avoid
- Anonymous reviews
- Vague praise ("They're great!")
- Reviews without business context

## Form Labels

### Use Clear, Natural Language
- "First Name" (not "Given Name")
- "Phone Number" (not "Contact Number")
- "Type of Property" (not "Service Category")
- "How Did You Hear About Us?" (not "Lead Source")

### Required Fields
Mark clearly with asterisk (*) and note at top of form.

### Error Messages
Be specific and helpful:
- "Please enter a valid email address" (not "Invalid input")
- "Phone number must be at least 10 digits" (not "Format error")

## Legal Pages

### Privacy Policy
- Plain language where possible
- Standard sections (collection, use, sharing, rights)
- Contact information for questions

### Terms of Service
- Clear service description
- Limitation of liability
- Governing law (Texas)

## SEO Considerations

### Page Titles
Format: `[Page Name] | 3MS Janitorial Services`

Example: `Commercial Cleaning Services | 3MS Janitorial Services`

### Meta Descriptions
- Include primary keyword
- Include location
- Include CTA language
- 150-160 characters

Example:
> Professional commercial cleaning for Greenville, TX businesses. Office, medical, retail cleaning. Insured & bonded. Request a free quote today!

### Headings
- One H1 per page (page title)
- H2 for major sections
- Include keywords naturally

## Content Updates

When updating content:
1. Maintain consistent voice
2. Update copyright year in footer
3. Keep service lists accurate
4. Refresh testimonials periodically
5. Update business hours if changed
